Our Facilities

Indoor Pitch

We boast the largest indoor hurling and football pitch in Wexford, an excellent facility for those cold winter nights.

Hurling Wall

Dedicated practice wall for improving skills and drills for all age groups.

Underage Pitch

A smaller floodlit pitch tailored for underage teams, promoting development at every level.
