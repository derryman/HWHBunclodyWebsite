Our Mission,Vision and Values

Mission

Aspires to be a modern, innovative, sustainable community-based and volunteer-led club, that provides a community-based outlet so that as many as possible participate in Gaelic games, Irish language and culture, for as long as possible.

Vision

To promote Gaelic games at all age levels, provide our club members and supporters with lifelong engagement in cultural and sporting activities and play a central and positive role in our local community.

Core Values

  • Respect
  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • One Club
  • Inclusiveness
  • Player welfare
  • Teamwork & Volunteerism
  • Commitment
  • Self driven Improvement
